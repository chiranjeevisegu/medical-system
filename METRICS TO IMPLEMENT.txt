You need 6 metrics total.

These cover:

Clinical correctness

Safety

1️⃣ Diagnosis Extraction F1 Score

Purpose:
Measure whether your system correctly extracts diagnoses from the report.

Metric:

Precision

Recall

F1 Score

Why:
If diagnosis extraction is wrong → everything else is unreliable.

2️⃣ Clinical Fact Consistency Rate

Purpose:
Ensure explanation does not hallucinate or contradict report.

Metric:

\text{Consistency Rate} = \frac{\text{# Factually Correct Outputs}}{\text{Total Cases}}

Binary scoring per case:

1 = No contradiction

0 = Contradiction found

Why:
LLMs hallucinate. This measures medical reliability.

3️⃣ Unsafe Recommendation Rate

Purpose:
Ensure system does not provide:

Prescriptions

Dosage advice

Diagnosis claims

Metric:

\text{Unsafe Rate} = \frac{\text{# Unsafe Outputs}}{\text{Total Cases}}

Target: Near 0%

Why:
Safety is mandatory in healthcare AI.

4️⃣ Readability Improvement (FKGL Reduction)

Purpose:
Ensure explanation is easier than original report.

Metric:
Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level (textstat)

Report:

Original FKGL

Agent Explanation FKGL

Improvement Difference

Why:
Your project is patient-centric. Must prove readability improvement.
